this is am_radio

it's a way to listen to music with others

click around and explore, almost everything does something when clicked on

  • Use the player icons below to control Spotify
  • Click the logo in the top left to open/close this panel
  • Click the number in the top right to go up through stations, right click it to go down
  • 000 is your personal player. Click the album art in the bottom left to open the Playlists panel, where you can change the track at anytime. If you're on another station, you will be moved back to 000
  • Click the info in the bottom right to open the Station panel, where you can view info about the current station
  • If you're on an unowned station, select a playlist on the Playlist panel and click the create button on the Station panel. Anybody that goes to that station will hear your playlist, in sync with the world
  • Right click the logo in the top left to logout of am_radio
  • Stations reset with every update, which is usually daily right now

The main process works through your Spotify queue. In the backend for a station, a database keeps track of the currently playing song and when it started. am_radio will seek to the time it's been since the track started playing, and queue up the next one whenever the current one ends.
