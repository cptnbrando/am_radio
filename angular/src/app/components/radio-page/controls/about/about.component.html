this is am_radio

it's a way to listen to music with others

  • Click the top bar to open/close this panel
  • Click the song name to open the Playlists panel
  • Click the number in the top right to go up through stations, right click (or hold) it to go down
  • You can also use the keyboard to do things
    • Use the number keys to change the station
    • Use the left and right arrow keys to change the track on 000
    • Use the up and down arrow keys to change the station
    • Use the 'S' key to open the Station panel, 'P' key to open Playlist panel
  • Click the bottom right to open the station panel
  • Click the visualizer to fullscreen it

Use the playlist and station panels to create a station

The main process works through your Spotify queue. In the backend for a station, a database keeps track of the currently playing song and when it started. am_radio will seek to the time it's been since the track started playing, and queue up the next one whenever the current one ends.
