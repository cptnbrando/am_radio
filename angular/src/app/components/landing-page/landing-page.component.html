welcome to am_radio

about


am_radio is a way to share and listen to Spotify playlists with others.

Log in with Spotify and the app will load in one of your recently played playlists. Click the now playing info in the bottom left corner to open up the playlists panel. Click through your playlists and click on any track to play it, or click the playlist name to play the entire playlist.

The top right corner lists the current playing station. Left click it to increment, or right click it to decrement. The app will go through numbers 000-999 and attempt to play the listed station, if one exists. If not, it will be free and you can create a new station from one of your playlists.

Simply click the station info in the bottom right corner and open the station panel. Then go back to the playlist panel and select a playlist. If it's valid, you should see a "create station" button. Click it, and a station will be created from your playlist, live for anybody on the internet to listen to.

Stations reset whenever I update the app, which is pretty much daily right now. I'm working on some cool ideas including a visualizer and a chat function (which is sorta kinda there if you left click the logo and use the text box). Right now, feel free to share your playlists and send them to friends, the music will be synced amongst all the listeners.
