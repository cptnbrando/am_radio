about


am_radio is a way to share and listen to Spotify playlists with others.

Log in and the app will play one of your recently played playlists. From there, you can listen to your own music or browse user created stations.

You can also chat with other users. Everything you hear will be in sync with everybody else.

You can create your own radio station from one of your playlists if the number is unowned. Anybody on that station will be listening in sync with you. Share it with friends and have a party ðŸŽ‰
